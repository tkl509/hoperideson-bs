Mission

Our mission, as a faith-based organization, is to utilize the healing and nurturing capabilities of horses with the goal of providing a higher level of happiness, belonging, collaboration and inclusion for all participants, no matter how broken, abused, neglected or discarded.
Although we are not offering therapy or counseling; we believe there are benefits from participating in a horsemanship program for children.
